I was educated in Beijing, Taiwan, Leiden, and Cambridge studying physics and cosmology. I am currently a student at the University of Sheffield in Gravitation and Cosmology Research Group.

Interests

Cosmology, gravity, galaxy, gravitational waves.

I am interested in models beyond ΛCDM and accelerated expansion of the Universe. There are two major research branches for understanding the Universe expansion, a new dark energy model, or a modified gravity theory. Enormous efforts have been made to study both of them in the past two decades. I focus on constraints from an observational side.
